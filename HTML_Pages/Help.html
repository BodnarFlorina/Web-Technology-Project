Frequently Asked Questions

Locked out of your HeMa account?

1.Open the HeMa app

2.On the main view of the app, tap "Sign in"

3.Tap "Forgot password?"

4.Enter the email address associated with your account

5.Tap "Send" and you will receive an email with a link to reset your password (make sure you check the junk or spam folder)

Here's how to add photos / images of your plants:

1.Tap the plus button in the bottom right corner in the main page (to do-list)

2.Select "Add task to a plant"

3.Pick plant

4.Tap button "Note & Picture"

5.Take a new picture with your camera or select an image from your photo library

6.Make sure "Use as plant head image" is selected

7.Save!
