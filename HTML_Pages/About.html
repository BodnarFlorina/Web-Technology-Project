About Hema

Hema is a Herbal Web Manager that offers support for the organization of collections of graphic representations of pressed plants and related attributes. Whether you are a botanist, a herbalist, or just a nature lover who likes to collect and preserve plant specimens, Hema provides a convenient and user-friendly platform to manage your herbal collection. With Hema, you can easily upload images of your pressed plants, add relevant information such as their scientific and common names, descriptions, locations, and dates of collection, and keep track of their attributes, properties, and uses. Join Hema today and start exploring the beauty and diversity of nature through the lens of herbalism!
